Visit Tips

  • 01
    Dress Code Cover shoulders & knees. No sleeveless tops or shorts inside.
  • 02
    Opening Hours Basilica: daily 07:00–19:00. Museums: Mon–Sat 09:00–18:00.
  • 03
    Book in Advance Buy tickets online to skip 2–3 hour walk-in queues.
  • 04
    Silence & Respect No flash photography. Keep voices low inside the Chapel.
